Place Your Order

PHONE:

To place a telephone order, call toll free 1-888-332-2668 between 8:00 a.m. and 5:30 p.m. Eastern time. (Please note: Indianapolis now uses Eastern Daylight Time from spring through fall.) FFA accepts credit cards and valid school purchase orders for telephone orders. Please make sure to have your credit card or valid school purchase order information ready when placing your order.

FAX:

To place a fax order, dial toll free 1-800-366-6556 – 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Please note: FFA can only accept valid school purchase orders for fax orders. Credit cards will not be accepted for fax orders.

MAIL ORDERS TO:

National FFA Organization
Attn: Accounts Receivable
P.O. Box 68960
Indianapolis, Indiana 46268-0960

E-MAIL:

An electronic printable order form is available online. E-mail the completed order form to orders@ffa.org. Payment for e-mail orders can be made with a valid school purchase order. For security reasons and to safeguard your privacy, FFA recommends that you do not pay with your credit card when e-mailing orders

* We have recently updated the Electronic/Printable Order Form. Please report any issues or concerns, as well as any other feedback regarding this form by emailing orders@ffa.org.

E-MAIL CONFIRMATION:

FFA will e-mail an order confirmation if an e-mail address has been provided. For Internet and e-mail orders, the order confirmation will be e-mailed automatically. For all other methods of ordering (telephone, fax and mail), it will be necessary for you to provide your e-mail address to FFA. Please take the time to review your order confirmation carefully as you will have just one business day to make any changes or corrections.

TRACKING ORDERS:

You can track the shipment status of your FFA order online at www.ups.com. Simply enter the UPS® tracking number from your order confirmation e-mail. If you are placing a telephone order, make sure to provide the FFA customer service representative with your e-mail address. Once your merchandise is shipped, you will automatically receive an e-mail confirmation of your order and a UPS tracking number.

Please allow a minimum of 24 hours for orders to be processed. Orders placed over weekends and holidays will be processed the next business day.

Payment Facts and Information

SCHOOL PURCHASE ORDERS:

FFA accepts valid school purchase orders as payment for:

  • Telephone Orders
  • Fax Orders (FFA advisor’s signature required)
  • Mail Orders (FFA advisor’s signature required)
  • Internet Orders
  • E-mail Orders

To begin using valid school purchase orders as a payment method, you must:

  • Be an established FFA chapter with a chapter ID number,
  • Be an FFA chapter advisor,
  • Be authorized by your school to use school purchase orders, and
  • Have an established Chapter Account with FFA.

To create a Chapter Account, go to http://www.ffa.org/shop and click on “Create Account.” Check “Yes” in the pull-down box to indicate that as an FFA chapter advisor, you are authorized to use school purchase orders; then add your chapter name and number along with the other required information. (You may also call FFA Customer Service at 1-888-332-2668 and speak to a customer service representative to establish a Chapter Account.) The FFA customer service staff will verify your information and send you an e-mail to confirm your Chapter Account status. Please allow up to five business days to receive your Chapter Account status.

With a Chapter Account, you will be able to use a valid school purchase order to pay for your orders; no credit card required. Please note: If you place an order at the same time you create your Chapter Account, you will need to pay for that order with a credit card. Or, you can open your Chapter Account without placing an order to allow time for your account to be activated for a future order. Also, orders paid by a valid school purchase order must be sent to the chapter’s “ship to” address that is on file with FFA. All invoices for items ordered with a valid school purchase order must be paid within 30 days of receipt. Accounts that are 90 days past due will be charged a $10 per month late fee.

CREDIT CARD ORDERS:

FFA accepts American Express, Discover, MasterCard and Visa credit cards for telephone, mail and Internet orders. Credit card orders will be sent to the shipping address given to the FFA customer service representative (for telephone orders) or provided on the order form (for mail and Internet orders). Please note: In addition to the credit card number, you may be asked to provide the cardholder’s name as it appears on the card, expiration date, security code, cardholder’s billing address and a daytime telephone number.

CHECK ORDERS:

Checks payable to “National FFA Organization” are accepted only for mail orders. A daytime telephone number must be included with the order. Allow three additional business days for check processing. FFA cannot accept checking account numbers for telephone or Internet orders. Please do not send cash.
